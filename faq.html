FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ticketseller?
Ticketseller is an online platform where you can buy tickets for various events, including sports, concerts, and theater shows.
How do I purchase tickets?
To purchase tickets, simply select the event you are interested in, choose your seats, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using a credit card or PayPal.
Can I get a refund for my tickets?
Refunds are not guaranteed, and they are subject to the specific policies of the event organizer. Please check the terms and conditions of your purchase carefully before completing your transaction.
What if I have problems with my tickets?
If you have any issues with your tickets, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible. We will do our best to assist you and resolve any problems.
Is my personal information safe on Ticketseller?
We take the security and privacy of our customers very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and financial information.

What happens if an event is cancelled or rescheduled?
If an event is cancelled, we will issue a refund for the full ticket price, including any service fees. If an event is rescheduled, your tickets will still be valid for the new date, unless otherwise stated by the event organizer.
Can I sell tickets on Ticketseller?
At this time, we do not offer a platform for individuals to sell their own tickets. However, we work with event organizers and ticket sellers to provide a wide selection of tickets for our customers.

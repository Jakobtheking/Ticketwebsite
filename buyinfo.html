How to Buy Tickets

Follow these simple steps to buy tickets for your favorite sports events:

  1. Select the event you want to attend from our Tickets page.
  2. Choose the date and time that works best for you.
  3. Select the type of ticket you want, and the number of tickets you want to purchase.
  4. Click the "Add to Cart" button to add the tickets to your shopping cart.
  5. When you're ready to check out, click the shopping cart icon in the top right corner of the screen.
  6. Review your order, and click the "Checkout" button.
  7. Enter your payment and contact information, and click the "Place Order" button.
  8. Once your order is processed, you will receive a confirmation email with your ticket details.

Ticket Prices

Our ticket prices vary depending on the event and the type of ticket you select. Please refer to the Tickets page for specific pricing information.
